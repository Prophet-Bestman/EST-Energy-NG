HVAC and Fabrication Works

The AKPO Expansion Tanks Rehabilitation Project executed by EST resolved the the problem of rust in the HVAC chilled water circuits. The rust and consequential murky waters were caused by dissolved air, used in pressurising the replaced tanks. More so, the nuisance popping of pressure relief valves (PRVs) due to pressure build up was mitigated with the bladder lined expansion tanks introduced for the essential, nonessential and topside HVAC chilled water circuits.

The project scope included extensive welding, fabrication and piping operations in hazardous zone classified areas, and we safely achieved a number of firsts for the FPSO

  • Welding on chilled water line
  • Hot tapping operations on process lines
  • Pipe freezing of the process line using CO2

In conjunction with our technical associates: HVAC & Refrigeration Engineering Ltd and Thermoplant Offshore Services UK, EST is able to provide expert HVAC solutions for industrial and commercial applications. Our service offering spans from manufacturing of offshore and marine HVAC units to the provision of maintenance services, support and critical spares.

Thermal Imaging

At EST, we understand that thermal imaging is not just about identifying hotspots. It is a systematic approach of data capture and analysis that enhances critical decision making. So we are meticulous in our delivery, ensuring optimal plant availability, and at low cost

Our engineers are able to capture insulator defects and oxidation of high voltage switches, imbalance loading and loose connections in electrical panels, overheated motors and misalignment issues, and so much more

Applications
  • High/ low voltage electrical lines, switch-gears, motors and transformer inspection
  • Gas leakage, pipes, insulation failure, and process flow detection
  • Product tank level
